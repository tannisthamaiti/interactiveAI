The 2 Types of AI: Specialist vs. Genius

So, it's really important to know the difference.

Narrow AI (What we have now): A master of ONE job. Like a calculator that's amazing at math but can't do anything else. All the AI you use today is Narrow AI.

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI): The original dream. An AI that can learn, reason, and be creative about ANYTHING, just like a human. This does not exist yet!
